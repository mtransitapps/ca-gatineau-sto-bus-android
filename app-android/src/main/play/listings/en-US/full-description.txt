This app adds Société de Transport de l'Outaouais (STO) buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from @STOGatineau on Twitter.

STO buses serve Gatineau in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-gatineau-sto-bus-android

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Société de Transport de l'Outaouais (STO).
https://www.sto.ca/en/business/developer-space-open-data/

This app is not related with the Société de Transport de l'Outaouais (STO).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read the news from Twitter
